Designed and built a CRM system tailored for the startup ecosystem, featuring deal pipeline tracking, contact management, and automated email outreach capabilities. The platform helps founders and sales teams manage investor relations and customer acquisition efficiently.

Implemented real-time pipeline updates using WebSockets and built automated workflow triggers for follow-up emails and task assignments. Integrated with popular email providers for seamless communication tracking.

The system reduced manual data entry by 70% and improved response rates by 25% through timely automated follow-ups and personalized outreach sequences.
