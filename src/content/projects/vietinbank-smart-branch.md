Built a digital banking interface for Vietinbank's smart branch initiative, featuring queue management, customer self-service kiosks, and staff dashboards. The system modernizes branch operations while maintaining strict banking security standards.

Implemented real-time queue management with WebSockets, allowing customers to book appointments and track wait times remotely. Built self-service interfaces for account inquiries, fund transfers, and document submissions.

Reduced average wait times by 40% and improved customer satisfaction scores by 25%. Enabled branch staff to handle 30% more customers daily through streamlined workflows and automated routine transactions.
