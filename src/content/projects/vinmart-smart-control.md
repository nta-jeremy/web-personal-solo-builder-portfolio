Engineered an inventory control system for Vinmart with smart reordering algorithms and real-time supply chain visibility. The system integrates with POS terminals, warehouse management, and supplier portals for end-to-end inventory tracking.

Implemented demand forecasting using historical sales data and seasonal patterns to optimize reorder points. Built real-time dashboards for inventory levels, expiry tracking, and supplier performance metrics.

Reduced stockouts by 35% and excess inventory by 20% through intelligent reordering algorithms. Improved supplier lead time visibility, enabling better procurement planning and cost optimization.
