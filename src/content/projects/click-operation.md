Built an internal operations dashboard for managing marketing campaigns and analyzing click-through metrics in real-time. The platform processes millions of click events daily and provides actionable insights for campaign optimization.

Leveraged ClickHouse for high-performance analytics queries on time-series data, and Go microservices for reliable event ingestion via Kafka. The frontend uses React with advanced data visualization components for intuitive metric exploration.

Enabled the marketing team to identify underperforming campaigns within minutes instead of hours, improving overall campaign ROI by 35%.
