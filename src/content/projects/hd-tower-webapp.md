Developed a corporate website and internal web application for HD Tower (Sun Group) to manage real estate projects and track sales performance. The platform serves both public-facing property listings and internal sales team tools.

Built responsive property listing pages with image galleries, virtual tour integration, and mortgage calculator. Implemented lead tracking and follow-up workflows for sales teams with automated email notifications.

Improved sales team productivity by 25% through centralized lead management and automated follow-up reminders. Reduced time-to-quote from 2 days to under 4 hours through automated pricing calculations.
