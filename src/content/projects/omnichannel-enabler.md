Built a unified omnichannel retail platform that seamlessly connects online stores, physical outlets, and warehouse systems. The platform enables real-time inventory synchronization, unified customer profiles, and consistent pricing across all touchpoints.

Key responsibilities included designing microservices architecture with Go and Node.js, implementing event-driven communication via Kafka, and optimizing database queries for 3000+ concurrent orders. Achieved 50% infrastructure cost reduction through efficient resource utilization and caching strategies with Redis.

The system now serves over 1 million registered customers with 99.9% uptime, processing thousands of orders daily while maintaining sub-100ms response times for critical paths.
