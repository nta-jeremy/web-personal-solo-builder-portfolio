Built a content management dashboard for GREENFEED GROUP to manage product catalogs, promotional campaigns, and user-generated content. The system provides role-based access control with approval workflows for content publishing.

Implemented rich text editing, image upload and optimization, and automated content moderation using Elasticsearch for full-text search across millions of content items. The dashboard supports multi-language content management and scheduled publishing.

Reduced content moderation workload by 50% through automated flagging and approval workflows. Enabled marketing teams to launch promotional campaigns in hours instead of days.
